When you use HolyFamily, we may collect the following types of information:

a. Information You Provide

  • Account information (name, email address, profile picture)

  • Content you create or schedule through our app

b. Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage data (how you interact with our application)

c. Information from Social Media Platforms

When you connect your social media accounts, we may receive:

  • Profile information from the platforms

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Schedule and publish content to your connected social media accounts

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With social media platforms to post content on your behalf

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information

Data Deletion Instructions

At HolyFamily, we respect your right to privacy and provide you with control over your personal data. This page explains how you can request deletion of your data from our systems.

How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data from HolyFamily, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to holyfamilypefct@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request"
  2. Include your registered email address and/or username in the email
  3. Specify whether you want to delete your entire account or specific data
  4. We will process your request within 30 days and send you a confirmation once completed

What Data We Delete

When you request data deletion, we will remove:

  • Your personal account information
  • Your connected social media account credentials
  • All scheduled posts and content
  • Any analytics data associated with your account
  • All other personal data stored in our systems

Important Notes

  • Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • We may retain certain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes
  • Deleting your data from HolyFamily does not affect content already published to social media platforms
  • You will need to separately remove app permissions from your social media accounts

Removing HolyFamily Access from Social Media Accounts

To completely remove HolyFamily's access to your social media accounts, you should also revoke access in each platform's settings:

Facebook/Instagram

  1. Go to your Facebook Settings & Privacy
  2. Click "Settings"
  3. Select "Apps and Websites"
  4. Find HolyFamily in the list and click "Remove"

Twitter

  1. Go to your Twitter Settings and Privacy
  2. Select "Apps and sessions"
  3. Click "Connected apps"
  4. Find HolyFamily and click "Revoke access"

Other Platforms

For other social media platforms, please check their respective settings sections for connected apps or third-party access.

If you have any questions about data deletion, please contact us at holyfamilypefct@gmail.com
